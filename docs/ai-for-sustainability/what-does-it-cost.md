## Reflection Prompts

- Which project step is likely to use the most compute, storage, or energy?
- Could the same question be answered with a smaller model, fewer runs, cached outputs, or shared data products?
- What costs are worth paying because they improve sustainability outcomes?
- What costs should be avoided because they add little value?
